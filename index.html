Photography

Aside from going to concerts, I love to take pictures. I prefer to use my DSLR (I have a Nikon D3300), but I can be pretty handy with a phone camera in a pinch. My favorite subjects are nature scenes, as well as action shots, particularly at the concerts I go to as well as the sporting events I attend. Additionaly, the radio station on the UM campus, WVUM 90.5, often has me take photos for them. Most of my photos wind up on my Instagram, but if they do not, they often appear elsewhere, such as my Twitter or even as the wallpaper on my computer.
